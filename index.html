Skills

Shopify Store Management

I'm experienced at optimising Shopify store structure and product setup, ensuring a seamless user experience that drives conversions. I also focus on tailoring product listings and categories to align with your marketing campaigns. Reporting & Forecasting

I specialise in analysing Shopify store performance, gathering data on sales trends, customer behavior, and other key metrics to provide actionable insights. On the forecasting side, I create auto-populating reporting documents that not only predict future trends but also help manage inventory efficiently and adapt to market changes.

Facebook Ads Set-up & Management

I have strong experience in Meta Ads Manager, running Facebook and Instagram ads. Google Ads & Analytics
